Welcome to LoL Data Rocket!

The project focused on gathering data from League of Legends matches and analyzing them in pursuit of some useful statistics. Here you can look at the data from hundreds of thousands LoL matches. 'What do I need this data for?' I hear you ask. Here are some ideas:

If you want to find the perfect (statisticly speaking) counter for your matchup, head for the Choose your counter section. There you can also look at the number of matches played in a certain matchup and evaluate for yourself what data is big enough to call the matchup 'winning'.


Maybe you wonder which champion is the most balanced of them all? The question still is pretty hard to answer but box plots in the Analysis section can help guide you in the correct direction.


Want to know more about the project and how it was developed? About section is the place to go.

Enjoy!
